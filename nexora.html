Nexora — Interview Preparation Platform

A focused guide for building a tailored interview preparation system, from MVP to a full-featured platform.

Project Overview

Solving real-world interview preparation challenges with smart technology.

The Problem

Interview prep is often generic and fails to target a candidate's specific skills and weaknesses, leading to stress and missed opportunities.

Our Solution

Nexora personalizes interview practice by generating questions directly from a user's resume and providing tailored feedback on their answers.

Project Milestones & Roadmap

A two-phase plan to build our platform, starting with a functional MVP.

Phase 1: MVP (2-Day Sprint)

Build a core, functional web system demonstrating the primary user flow: resume upload → tailored questions → text interview → basic feedback.

Phase 2: Full-Featured System

Transform the MVP into a smart, scalable platform with dynamic question generation, semantic answer analysis, user dashboards, and advanced reporting.
